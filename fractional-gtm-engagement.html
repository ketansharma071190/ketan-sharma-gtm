How it works

The first month is designed to find the truth, then move.

Week 1

Customer, lost-deal, founder, and team interviews

No assumptions. I inspect the deals, calls, CRM, messaging, current outbound, pricing, and team reality.

Week 2

ICP locked, pipeline audited, gaps exposed

We identify who actually buys, where deals stall, what is fantasy pipeline, and what needs to be fixed first.

Week 3

Playbook v1 and outbound motion live

Qualification, discovery, objection handling, CRM discipline, and outbound sequences begin running. Not perfect. Moving.

Week 4

Operating review

We review what is working and what is not. Real pipeline signal usually starts showing in week 6-8, not before.

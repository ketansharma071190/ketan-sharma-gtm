Decision

map my crop

Sell accuracy as a pricing weapon, alongside the mandated 30%.

Decision

Buyer

Price
SaaS + data licence, roughly ₹1 to 5 cr / yr, priced in loss-ratio basis points, not per acre.
The pilot + the ROI
  • Pre-bid: score 15 to 20 candidate clusters on modelled loss cost, so they bid sharp on winners and walk from the adverse-selected ones.
  • In-season: fortnightly yield-trajectory dashboards to reserve and reinsure early, and an audit-grade yield ready to defend claims.

Why it dwarfs the farmer price: a 2-point loss-ratio improvement on a ₹1,000 cr crop book is ~₹20 cr, against a ₹2 cr contract. ~10x, and it is their number, not ours. (Illustrative.)

08 · The pricing shift
map my crop

Stop pricing the map. Price the accuracy delta.

Three models, let the buyer self-select
  • Empanelment — per insurance-unit, government rate card. Thin margin, national volume, the reference logo.
  • Insurer / lender SaaS + data — ₹1 to 5 cr / yr, priced in loss-ratio or NPA basis points. Where the money is.
  • Outcome / success fee — a base plus a share of the loss-ratio or default reduction we drive. Highest trust, highest value.
Worked example (illustrative)
Farmer advisory today~₹3.5k / farmer / yr
Insurer data + SaaS contract~₹2 cr / yr
2-pt loss-ratio gain on a ₹1,000 cr book~₹20 cr
Value to the buyer vs our fee~10x

The same yield call, sold to whoever carries the risk, is worth 10 to 100x. Every basis point of accuracy compounds across a crore-account book. (Illustrative; the real numbers are theirs, pulled in the pilot.)

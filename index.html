What I fix

The operating system behind enterprise sales.

01

ICP and qualification

Using your real won, lost, stalled and founder-saved deals to define who buys, why, what disqualifies them, and where to stop early.

02

Sales playbook

Founder context turned into a usable system: discovery, objection handling, proof, pricing logic, stakeholder control, close plans a new hire can run.

03

Pipeline and CRM truth

Stage definitions, next-step discipline, deal inspection and forecast quality, so nobody has to ask ten people what is real.

04

Pricing and packaging

Whether deal size is capped by value, buyer level, packaging or fear. At ZenAdmin, ACV expanded 300% by changing pricing and who we sold to.

05

Hiring and coaching

Define the first roles properly, run the process, onboard, coach live deals, and make sure reps inherit judgment, not just a deck.

06

Founder handoff

By month 3 to 6, a documented system, a working pipeline, a clear cadence, and a team that decides without founder rescue.

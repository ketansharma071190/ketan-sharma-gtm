What I fix

The operating system behind enterprise sales.

01

ICP and qualification

02

Sales playbook

I pull founder context into a usable system: discovery questions, objection handling, proof points, pricing logic, stakeholder control, and close plans a new hire can use.

03

Pipeline and CRM truth

I rebuild stage definitions, next-step discipline, deal inspection, source visibility, and forecast quality so the founder no longer has to ask ten people what is real.

Founder handoff

The first 30 days

No assumptions. No theatre. Find the motion that is actually running.

Week 1

Customer, lost-deal, founder, and team interviews

I read the room before rewriting it: customers, prospects, lost deals, reps, founders, CRM, decks, calls, pricing, and current outbound.

Week 2

ICP and pipeline audit

We lock the highest-probability buyer patterns, expose stage leakage, kill fantasy pipeline, and identify the sales constraints that matter first.

Week 3

Playbook and outbound motion v1

The first working version goes live: qualification, discovery, messaging, CRM hygiene, objection map, and outbound sequences. Not perfect. Moving.

Week 4

Operating review and build plan

We review what is working and what is not. Real pipeline signal usually starts showing around week 6-8, not week 2, and anyone promising otherwise is selling theatre.

07 · The method
yuj
A productized method YUJ can own

The Trust Ladder. Every AI deployment has to climb it. YUJ designs the climb.

Rung 1Opaque
A black box. Users distrust it, ignore it, or route around it.
Rung 2Explainable
Design shows the why. Confidence, sources, reasoning made legible.
Rung 3Controllable
Humans can steer, correct, override. The off-ramp is designed.
Rung 4Accountable
Audit, escalation, responsibility are clear. Safe at scale.
Rung 5Trusted
Adopted, relied on, expanded. The AI investment finally pays back.
Bar height = human trust & adoption →  ·  the design interventions are the rungs  ·  it is the teardown, the engagement scope, and the quarterly metric, all at once
Signature method · used across this planTrust, made designable and measurable
